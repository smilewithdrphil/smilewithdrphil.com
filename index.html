Dr. Phillip Platt is a practicing general dentist at Prairie Creek Dental in Rogers, Arkansas — and an Invisalign Speaker for Align Technology. He started his Invisalign journey in 2023 with zero clear aligner experience. Today he teaches GPs across the country how to build predictable Invisalign systems, identify more candidates, and actually enjoy the process.

His message is simple: the learning curve is shorter than you think, and the right community makes all the difference.

Relatable Trajectory

Mid-career GP. No ortho background. Built a thriving Invisalign practice in a market of 80,000. If it worked there, it can work anywhere.

Systems Over Talent

The message isn’t “be better.” It’s “build the right systems.” Doctor flow, hygiene conversation, TC handoff, ClinCheck protocol — all of it is teachable.

Community-Proven

Not just theory. Active in Molis Coaching, on the ClinCheck Support Team, reviewing curriculum. The reps come from real cases, not keynote prep.

Builder’s Mindset

Named Invisalign Speaker by Align Technology. Also built his own software tools to solve problems his own practice faced. Thinks like an educator and an innovator.

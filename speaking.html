Six sessions. One system.

Each session is a standalone 2-hour lecture designed for 10–20 GPs at any experience level. No handouts required. No role-play. Just clear, actionable frameworks you can use Monday morning.

Session 1

Every Patient. Every Time.

The systems that make Invisalign a consistent part of every hygiene visit and new patient exam — before a single case starts.

Session 2

Invisalign Is a Team Sport

How hygiene, treatment coordinators, and chairside assistants each own a piece of the Invisalign conversation — and what breaks down when they don’t.

Session 3

Consistent ClinChecks for Consistent Outcomes

A repeatable ClinCheck review protocol that reduces surprises mid-treatment and builds confidence case after case.

Session 4

Know Your Lane. Then Widen It.

The most common mistakes GPs make in Invisalign — and a framework for expanding your skillset without overreaching on complexity.

Session 5

Deep Bites Done Right

Overcorrection protocol, clinical setup decisions, and the mindset shift that makes deep bite cases feel predictable instead of scary.

Session 6

Open Bites & Crossbites Are Easier Than You Think

A full treatment framework for two case types most GPs avoid — and why the outcomes are more predictable than their reputation suggests.

Format & Logistics

  • 2-hour lecture format
  • 10–20 doctors (small group study clubs)
  • Mixed or unknown experience levels welcome
  • Available for regional Align study clubs, private group events, and dental society CE programs

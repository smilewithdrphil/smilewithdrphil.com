Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2026

This privacy policy describes how smilewithdrphil.com (“the Site”) collects, uses, and protects information submitted by visitors.

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on this Site, we collect the following information: your name, email address, practice location or city, preferred event format, and any message content you provide. This information is submitted voluntarily and is used solely to respond to your inquiry about speaking engagements.

How Your Data Is Processed

Contact form submissions are processed through Google Forms, a service provided by Google LLC. By submitting the contact form, your data is transmitted to and stored by Google in accordance with their privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy. Form responses are accessible only to the site owner.

Analytics

This Site does not currently use analytics or tracking tools. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect the change.

Cookies

This Site does not set first-party cookies. Third-party services (such as Google Fonts) may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Information collected through the contact form is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry and facilitate potential speaking engagements.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and complete any related follow-up. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, please contact us at the email address below.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact: smilewithdrphil@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
